Welcome! We invite you to join us at the Cornerstone Café. We offer classic American staples with a modern twist. We believe that being a member of the community is both a privilege and a responsibility, which is why use only locally sourced ingredients from small family farms. Come relax in our beautiful, café, which is on the National Register of Historic Places. Enjoy the unique architecture inside, or take in the sun on our patio. We offer takeout and catering as well, so give us a call whether you need a night off from cooking or a picnic for 50.
